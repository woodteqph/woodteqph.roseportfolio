Company Profile

WOODTEQPH PRIME CORPORATION - Leading the way in premium construction solutions

Background

WOODTEQPH PRIME CORPORATION emerged in response to the growing demand for special setup designs in Architectural Glass and aluminum windows. Originally established as WOODTEQ WOOD PRODUCTS MANUFACTURING in March 2022, the company has evolved to include a broader scope, incorporating specializations in modular cabinetry and countertop installation.

This expansion is driven by the collaboration of strong technical skills, effective management, and strategic marketing approaches.

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To deliver high-quality kitchen cabinets, wardrobes, aluminum glass doors and window products that bring our customers' visions to life, while nurturing employee growth, contributing to the nation's development, and creating lasting value for our communities.

Our Vision

To be the leading provider of quality craftsmanship and innovation in the country, transforming homes and empowering employees to grow, while making a positive impact on the country's economic and social development.
